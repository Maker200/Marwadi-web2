Programme Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

Programme Specific Outcomes (PSOs) at Marwadi University vary by department and align with their Outcome-Based Education (OBE) framework. Below are the PSOs for major departments:

Faculty of Technology (Engineering)

  • Computer Science & Engineering (B.Tech)focuses on developing skills in designing and developing secure, efficient, and scalable software systems and applying emerging technologies responsibly to create innovative solutions.
  • Civil Engineeringprovides knowledge and skills for analysis, design, estimation, and software tools, along with the ability to assess construction materials in the field and laboratory.
  • Mechanical Engineeringincludes emerging fields such as IoT, Advanced Robotics, and Additive Manufacturing, aiming to foster student success, research, and economic contributions

Faculty of Management (MBA)

  • PSO1:Adapt to environmental changes and understand societal and ecological issues in management.
  • PSO2: Function effectively in teams with good interpersonal skills, ethical conduct, and social responsibility.
  • Effectively utilize technology in business.

Bachelor of Business 4dministration (BBA)

  • Learning Outcomes (PLOs) for BBA include defining and analyzing business problems,demonstrating competencein domestic and global business, and developing the ability to work effectively in diverse teams.
